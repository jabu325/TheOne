Website editor

Your website comes complete with a powerful yet easy to use Website Editor. This system enables you to make changes to all the text and images on your website after you have launched. This includes things like posting new blog articles, updating your about us page, adding a new banner image, adding gallery images of your recent work etc.

The Enhanced Website Editor adds additional functionality and controls to your content management system. In addition to the above you'll get access to reports so you can monitor your website's performance, extra control to make your own page layouts and the ability to add and remove unlimited pages of all types.

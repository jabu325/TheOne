Page sections

Page sections are the building blocks of your website. A page section can contain one type of content. Therefore the more page sections your package comes with, the more information you can have on your page.

Available page sections types:
  • Text - A section of text with a title.
  • Text with image - A section of text with an image next to it.
  • Image - An image with a title.
  • Gallery - A collection of up to 10 images.
  • Video - A video from YouTube or Vimeo.
  • Testimonials - A collection of testimonials.
  • Contact form - A basic form where people can contact you.
  • Map - An interactive map with a location pin.
  • Contact details - Provide your contact details e.g. telephone, email, address etc.

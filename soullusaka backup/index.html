OUR PRESTIGIOUS VENUES

At Sounds of Underground Lusaka, the experience is just as much about the setting as it is about the music. Each show is carefully curated to take place in a venue that complements the event’s intimate, high-calibre atmosphere. Rather than being tied to a single location, Sounds of Underground Lusaka moves across the city, bringing music lovers together in some of Lusaka’s most refined spaces. Past shows have unfolded in the sophisticated ambience of Bodega and the elegant setting of Taste Restaurant, each offering an immersive experience where guests can fully connect with the sound, the artists, and each other. For the upcoming edition, we are excited to host Sounds of Underground Lusaka at The Gathering—a space known for its warm, stylish, and intimate atmosphere. Perfectly aligned with the event’s ethos, The Gathering will provide the ideal backdrop for a night of unforgettable performances, world-class entertainment, and deep musical appreciation. Lusaka, with its rich cultural heartbeat and vibrant creative scene, is the perfect city for such an evolving, exclusive experience. Whether in an upscale restaurant, a hidden gem of a venue, or a sophisticated lounge, every Sounds of Underground Lusaka location is designed to transform live music into something truly special. Get ready for an extraordinary night at The Gathering—where music, ambience, and artistry come together in perfect harmony

All That Jazz...!

An Unforgettable Celebration of Music, Culture & Good Vibes . Get ready for a one-of-a-kind musical experience where Zambia’s finest artists—both legendary icons and rising stars—share the stage for a night of pure, soulful artistry. Sounds of Underground Lusaka is not just a concert; it’s a gathering of music lovers, creatives, and culture enthusiasts coming together to celebrate live music in its rawest, most authentic form. Expect an electrifying mix of Afro-jazz, soul, and contemporary African sounds, performed by some of Lusaka’s most exceptional talents. The music will move you, the atmosphere will inspire you, and the company will make it all the more unforgettable. Of course, no great experience is complete without delicious food, refreshing drinks, and plenty of good vibes. Whether you're swaying to the rhythm, indulging in great flavours, or simply soaking in the energy of the night, this is an event that promises to leave you uplifted. Come with your friends, bring your energy, and immerse yourself in a night of top-tier entertainment, culture, and connection—all in a setting designed for music to be truly heard and felt.

A Culinary Journey Like No Other

Prepare your palate for an exquisite fusion of flavours, where bold African spices meet refined continental cuisine in a feast designed to excite and delight. From reimagined classics to daring new fusions, every dish is a celebration of creativity and culture. Of course, no meal is complete without the perfect drink in hand. Sip on the continent’s finest wines, homegrown brews, and expertly crafted spirits—each selection carefully curated to elevate your experience. Whether you're a foodie, a drink connoisseur, or simply here for a good time, you’ll be spoiled for choice. Come hungry, come thirsty, and get ready to savour every sip and bite.
